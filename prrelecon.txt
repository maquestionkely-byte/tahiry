    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "proposition relative" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>proposition relative</strong> dia fehezanteny (proposition) izay <strong>mamaritra</strong> ny anarana (nom) na ny pronom teo alohany.<br><br>
      🔹 Mitovy amin'ny hoe <strong>"izay"</strong> amin'ny teny malagasy.<br><br>
      🔹 Misy <strong>pronom relatif</strong> (mpamari-toetra) izay manomboka azy :<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-qui">QUI</span> → misolo ny <strong>sujet</strong> (mpanao)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>L'homme <span class="color-qui">qui</span> parle est mon père.</em> (Ilay lehilahy <span class="color-qui">izay</span> miteny dia raiko.)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-que">QUE</span> → misolo ny <strong>COD</strong> (Complément d'Objet Direct)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>Le livre <span class="color-que">que</span> je lis est intéressant.</em> (Ilay boky <span class="color-que">izay</span> vakiko dia mahaliana.)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-ou">OÙ</span> → misolo ny <strong>lieu</strong> (toerana) na le <strong>temps</strong> (fotoana)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>La maison <span class="color-ou">où</span> j'habite est grande.</em> (Ilay trano <span class="color-ou">izay</span> ipetrahako dia lehibe.)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-dont">DONT</span> → misolo ny <strong>de + nom</strong> (COI - Complément d'Objet Indirect)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>Le livre <span class="color-dont">dont</span> je parle est intéressant.</em> (Ilay boky <span class="color-dont">izay</span> resahiko dia mahaliana.)
    </div>

  <!-- 1. QUI - Sujet -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ <span class="color-qui">QUI</span> (sujet - mpanao)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <span class="color-qui">QUI</span> dia ampiasaina hisolo ny <strong>sujet</strong> (mpanao) ao amin'ny proposition relative.<br><br>
      <strong>Règle :</strong><br>
      • <span class="color-qui">QUI</span> + <strong>verbe</strong> (miaraka amin'ny sujet)<br>
      • Manondro olona na zavatra<br>
      Ohatra : <em>L'homme <span class="color-qui">qui</span> parle.</em> (Ilay lehilahy <span class="color-qui">izay</span> miteny.)
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">L'homme <span class="color-qui">qui</span> parle est mon père. (Ilay lehilahy <span class="color-qui">izay</span> miteny dia raiko.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">La femme <span class="color-qui">qui</span> chante est chanteuse. (Ilay vehivavy <span class="color-qui">izay</span> mihira dia mpihira.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Le chat <span class="color-qui">qui</span> dort est noir. (Ilay saka <span class="color-qui">izay</span> matory dia mainty.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">La voiture <span class="color-qui">qui</span> est rouge est à moi. (Ilay fiara <span class="color-qui">izay</span> mena dia ahy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Les enfants <span class="color-qui">qui</span> jouent sont heureux. (Ireo ankizy <span class="color-qui">izay</span> milalao dia faly.)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> <span class="color-qui">QUI</span> + <strong>verbe</strong>. <span class="color-qui">QUI</span> dia misolo ny <strong>sujet</strong> (mpanao).
