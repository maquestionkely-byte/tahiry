    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "formation des mots" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>formation des mots</strong> dia mamaritra ny <strong>fomba famoronana</strong> teny vaovao amin'ny teny frantsay.<br><br>
      🔹 Misy karazany <strong>roa</strong> lehibe ny teny :<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Mots simples</strong> → teny tsy azo zaraina intsony.<br>
      Ohatra : <em>jour</em> (andro), <em>terre</em> (tany), <em>maison</em> (trano)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Mots construits</strong> → teny noforonina avy amin'ny fototeny (racine).<br><br>
      🔹 Misy fomba <strong>telu</strong> hamoronana mots construits :<br><br>
      <span class="icone">🔧</span> <strong>Préfixation</strong> → manampy <span class="color-prefixe">préfixe</span> (eo aloha)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>refaire</em> (manao indray) ← <em>re- + faire</em><br><br>
      <span class="icone">🔧</span> <strong>Suffixation</strong> → manampy <span class="color-suffixe">suffixe</span> (eo aoriana)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>jardinier</em> (mpamboly) ← <em>jardin + -ier</em><br><br>
      <span class="icone">🔧</span> <strong>Composition</strong> → manambatra teny <strong>roa</strong> na <strong>mihoatra</strong><br>
      Ohatra : <em>portefeuille</em> (kitapo) ← <em>porte + feuille</em>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ MOTS SIMPLES</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>mot simple</strong> dia teny <strong>tsy azo zaraina</strong> ho singa kely kokoa. Izy no <strong>fototeny</strong> (racine) izay ampiasaina hamoronana teny hafa.<br><br>
      <strong>Ohatra :</strong><br>
      • <em>jour</em> (andro)<br>
      • <em>terre</em> (tany)<br>
      • <em>maison</em> (trano)<br>
      • <em>voir</em> (mahita)<br>
      • <em>beau</em> (tsara)
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Le <strong>jour</strong> est beau. (Tsara ny <strong>andro</strong>.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">La <strong>terre</strong> est ronde. (Bory ny <strong>tany</strong>.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Cette <strong>maison</strong> est grande. (Lehibe ity <strong>trano</strong> ity.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Je <strong>vois</strong> un oiseau. (M<strong>ahita</strong> vorona aho.)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny mots simples dia <strong>fototeny</strong> (racines) izay ampiasaina hamoronana mots construits.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 2. PRÉFIXATION -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>2️⃣ PRÉFIXATION (manampy eo aloha)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>préfixation</strong> dia fomba famoronana teny amin'ny alalan'ny fanampiana <strong>préfixe</strong> (tovy kely) <strong>eo alohan'ny</strong> fototeny (racine).<br><br>
      <strong>Préfixes courants :</strong><br>
      • <strong>re-</strong> → indray (refaire = manao indray)<br>
      • <strong>dé-</strong> → manala (défaire = manala / manavotra)<br>
      • <strong>pré-</strong> → aloha (prévoir = mahita mialoha)<br>
      • <strong>in-</strong> / <strong>im-</strong> / <strong>il-</strong> / <strong>ir-</strong> → tsy (incapable = tsy mahay)<br>
      • <strong>mal-</strong> → ratsy (malade = marary)<br>
      • <strong>co-</strong> → miaraka (copain = namana)<br>
      • <strong>trans-</strong> → miampita (transporter = mitondra)<br>
      • <strong>anti-</strong> → manohitra (antigel = manohitra ny ranomandry)
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>re-</strong> + faire → <strong>refaire</strong> (manao indray)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>dé-</strong> + faire → <strong>défaire</strong> (manala / manavotra)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>pré-</strong> + voir → <strong>prévoir</strong> (mahita mialoha)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>in-</strong> + capable → <strong>incapable</strong> (tsy mahay)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>mal-</strong> + ade → <strong>malade</strong> (marary)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>co-</strong> + pain → <strong>copain</strong> (namana)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny préfixe dia <strong>manova ny hevitry</strong> ny fototeny fa tsy manova ny sokajin-teny (verbe, nom, adjectif) matetika.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 3. SUFFIXATION -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>3️⃣ SUFFIXATION (manampy eo aoriana)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>suffixation</strong> dia fomba famoronana teny amin'ny alalan'ny fanampiana <strong>suffixe</strong> (tovy kely) <strong>eo aorian'ny</strong> fototeny (racine).<br><br>
      <strong>Suffixes courants :</strong><br>
      • <strong>-ier</strong> → olona manao asa (jardinier = mpamboly)<br>
      • <strong>-euse</strong> / <strong>-rice</strong> → olona vavy manao asa (danseuse = mpandihy vavy)<br>
      • <strong>-ment</strong> → fomba (lentement = mora)<br>
      • <strong>-tion</strong> / <strong>-ation</strong> → hetsika (information = fampahalalana)<br>
      • <strong>-able</strong> / <strong>-ible</strong> → azo atao (mangeable = azo hanina)<br>
      • <strong>-eur</strong> → olona manao asa (chanteur = mpihira)<br>
      • <strong>-iste</strong> → olona manao asa (artiste = mpanakanto)<br>
      • <strong>-ment</strong> → fomba (doucement = mora)<br>
      • <strong>-té</strong> → toetra (beauté = hatsarana)<br>
      • <strong>-age</strong> → hetsika (voyage = dia)
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">jardin + <strong>-ier</strong> → <strong>jardinier</strong> (mpamboly)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">danse + <strong>-euse</strong> → <strong>danseuse</strong> (mpandihy vavy)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">lent + <strong>-ment</strong> → <strong>lentement</strong> (mora)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">information + <strong>-tion</strong> → <strong>information</strong> (fampahalana)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">mange + <strong>-able</strong> → <strong>mangeable</strong> (azo hanina)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">chante + <strong>-eur</strong> → <strong>chanteur</strong> (mpihira)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny suffixe dia <strong>manova ny sokajin-teny</strong> matetika (ohatra : verbe → nom, adjectif → adverbe).
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 4. COMPOSITION -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>4️⃣ COMPOSITION (manambatra teny roa)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>composition</strong> dia fomba famoronana teny amin'ny alalan'ny fanambarana <strong>teny roa</strong> na <strong>mihoatra</strong> mba hamoronana teny vaovao.<br><br>
      <strong>Ohatra :</strong><br>
      • <em>porte + feuille</em> → <strong>portefeuille</strong> (kitapo)<br>
      • <em>garde + robe</em> → <strong>garderobe</strong> (efitrano fitafiana)<br>
      • <em>chou + fleur</em> → <strong>chou-fleur</strong> (voatavo)<br>
      • <em>pomme + terre</em> → <strong>pomme de terre</strong> (ovo)<br>
      • <em>arc + en + ciel</em> → <strong>arc-en-ciel</strong> (avana)<br>
      • <em>sans + soin</em> → <strong>insouciant</strong> (tsy miraharaha) - miaraka amin'ny préfixe
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay

    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny composition dia miteraka teny vaovao amin'ny alalan'ny fanambarana <strong>teny roa</strong> na <strong>mihoatra</strong>.
    </div>
  </div>
