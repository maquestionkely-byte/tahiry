  <!-- 1. NUANCE D'INTENSITÉ - Hamafiny -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ <span class="color-intensite">NUANCE D'INTENSITÉ</span> (hamafiny)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <span class="color-intensite">nuance d'intensité</span> dia mampiseho ny <strong>hamafiny</strong> (degré) samy hafa eo amin'ny teny mitovy hevitra.<br><br>
      <strong>Exemples :</strong>
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">fatigué (vizana) → épuisé (reraka tanteraka) → <strong>épuisé</strong> dia <span class="color-intensite">mahery kokoa</span> noho ny <strong>fatigué</strong>.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">content (faly kely) → heureux (faly be) → ravi (faly indrindra)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">froid (mangatsiaka) → glacial (mangatsiaka be)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">petit (kely) → minuscule (kely dia kely)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">chaud (mafana) → brûlant (mafana be)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <span class="color-intensite">nuance d'intensité</span> dia mampiseho ny <strong>hamafiny</strong> (degré) samy hafa.<br>
      Ohatra : <em>fatigué</em> (vizana) < <em>épuisé</em> (reraka tanteraka) < <em>exténué</em> (reraka indrindra)
    </div>
  </div>

      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-registre">SOUTENU</span> : dormir (matory) → <span class="color-registre">COURANT</span> : dormir (matory) → <span class="color-registre">FAMILIER</span> : pioncer (matory)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-registre">SOUTENU</span> : parler (miresaka) → <span class="color-registre">COURANT</span> : parler (miresaka) → <span class="color-registre">FAMILIER</span> : causer (miresaka)</span>
