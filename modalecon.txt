  <!-- Carte : Inona ny modalisation ? -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "modalisation" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>modalisation</strong> dia ny fomba <strong>anehoana ny hevitra</strong> (point de vue) na ny <strong>fihetseham-po</strong> (sentiment) ananan'ny mpandahateny na mpanoratra.<br><br>
      🔹 Mitovy amin'ny hoe <strong>"fanehoana hevitra"</strong> amin'ny teny malagasy.<br><br>
      🔹 Misy marika <strong>efatra</strong> ahafahana manao modalisation :<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-sujet">PRONOMS PERSONNELS</span> → mampiasa <strong>"je"</strong>, <strong>"tu"</strong>, <strong>"nous"</strong> mba hanehoana fandraisana anjaran'ny mpandahateny.<br>
      Ohatra : <em><span class="color-sujet">Je</span> pense que c'est une bonne idée.</em><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-verbe">VERBES MODAUX</span> → mampiasa <strong>pouvoir</strong>, <strong>devoir</strong>, <strong>vouloir</strong> mba hanehoana fahalalahana, adidy, na faniriana.<br>
      Ohatra : <em>Tu <span class="color-verbe">dois</span> venir.</em><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-adverbe">ADVERBES MODAUX</span> → mampiasa <strong>peut-être</strong>, <strong>sans doute</strong>, <strong>certainement</strong> mba hanehoana fahalalahana na fisalasalana.<br>
      Ohatra : <em><span class="color-adverbe">Peut-être</span> qu'il viendra.</em><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <span class="color-mode">MODES VERBAUX</span> → mampiasa ny <strong>conditionnel</strong> na <strong>subjonctif</strong> mba hanehoana faniriana na fisalasalana.<br>
      Ohatra : <em>Je <span class="color-mode">voudrais</span> un café.</em>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 1. PRONOMS PERSONNELS - Fandraisana anjara -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ <span class="color-sujet">PRONOMS PERSONNELS</span> (fandraisana anjara)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <span class="color-sujet">PRONOMS PERSONNELS</span> dia mampiseho fa <strong>mandray anjara</strong> (s'implique) ny mpandahateny ao amin'ny resaka.<br><br>
      <strong>Pronoms utilisés :</strong><br>
      • <span class="color-sujet">Je</span> (izaho) → fandraisana anjaran'ny mpandahateny<br>
      • <span class="color-sujet">Tu</span> (ianao) → fandraisana anjaran'ny olona iresahana<br>
      • <span class="color-sujet">Nous</span> (izahay) → fandraisana anjaran'ny vondrona
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-sujet">Je</span> pense que c'est une bonne idée. (Mieritreritra aho fa hevitra tsara izany.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-sujet">Je</span> crois qu'il a raison. (Mino aho fa marina izy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-sujet">Nous</span> pensons que c'est important. (Mieritreritra izahay fa zava-dehibe izany.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-sujet">Tu</span> trouves que c'est bien ? (Mahita ve ianao fa tsara izany?)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <span class="color-sujet">PRONOMS PERSONNELS</span> dia mampiseho fa <strong>mandray anjara</strong> (s'implique) ny mpandahateny.<br>
      Ohatra : <em><span class="color-sujet">Je</span> pense que c'est bien.</em>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 2. VERBES MODAUX - Fahalalahana, adidy, faniriana -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>2️⃣ <span class="color-verbe">VERBES MODAUX</span> (fahalalahana, adidy, faniriana)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <span class="color-verbe">VERBES MODAUX</span> dia mampiseho <strong>fahalalahana</strong> (pouvoir), <strong>adidy</strong> (devoir), na <strong>faniriana</strong> (vouloir).<br><br>
      <strong>Verbes utilisés :</strong><br>
      • <span class="color-verbe">Pouvoir</span> → fahalalahana (afaka)<br>
      • <span class="color-verbe">Devoir</span> → adidy (tokony / tsy maintsy)<br>
      • <span class="color-verbe">Vouloir</span> → faniriana (te-hanao)
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Tu <span class="color-verbe">peux</span> venir si tu veux. (Afaka ho avy ianao raha te-hanao.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Tu <span class="color-verbe">dois</span> venir maintenant. (Tokony ho avy ianao izao.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Je <span class="color-verbe">veux</span> un café. (Te-hisotro kafe aho.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Il <span class="color-verbe">peut</span> réussir s'il travaille. (Afaka hahomby izy raha miasa.)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <span class="color-verbe">VERBES MODAUX</span> dia mampiseho <strong>fahalalahana</strong> (pouvoir), <strong>adidy</strong> (devoir), na <strong>faniriana</strong> (vouloir).<br>
      Ohatra : <em>Tu <span class="color-verbe">dois</span> venir.</em>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 3. ADVERBES MODAUX - Fisalasalana, fahalalahana -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>3️⃣ <span class="color-adverbe">ADVERBES MODAUX</span> (fisalasalana, fahalalahana)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <span class="color-adverbe">ADVERBES MODAUX</span> dia mampiseho <strong>fisalasalana</strong> (doute) na <strong>fahalalahana</strong> (probabilité).<br><br>
      <strong>Adverbes utilisés :</strong><br>
      • <span class="color-adverbe">Peut-être</span> → mety ho<br>
      • <span class="color-adverbe">Sans doute</span> → azo antoka fa<br>
      • <span class="color-adverbe">Certainement</span> → tena marina<br>
      • <span class="color-adverbe">Probablement</span> → mety ho<br>
      • <span class="color-adverbe">Apparemment</span> → toa
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny frantsay
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-adverbe">Peut-être</span> qu'il viendra demain. (Mety ho avy rahampitso izy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-adverbe">Sans doute</span>, il a raison. (Azo antoka fa marina izy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Il <span class="color-adverbe">certainement</span> gagnera. (Tena hahomby izy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><span class="color-adverbe">Probablement</span>, il est fatigué. (Mety ho vizana izy.)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <span class="color-adverbe">ADVERBES MODAUX</span> dia mampiseho <strong>fisalasalana</strong> (doute) na <strong>fahalalahana</strong> (probabilité).<br>
      Ohatra : <em><span class="color-adverbe">Peut-être</span> qu'il viendra.</em>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 4. MODES VERBAUX - Faniriana, fisalasalana -->

      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Il est important que tu <span class="color-mode">sois</span> là. (Zava-dehibe ny ho teo ianao.)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <span class="color-mode">MODES VERBAUX</span> dia mampiseho <strong>faniriana</strong> (conditionnel) na <strong>fisalasalana</strong> (subjonctif).<br>
